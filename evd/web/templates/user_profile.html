    Frequently Asked Questions
       I can read and write my native language, but have done my education in English medium and not conversant with the technical subject terms. Wont that be a problem?
    Ans: Passion and commitment along with linguistic ability to read and write, we have observed most of the teachers in the past were able to manage quite comfortably to teach in local language. To support, both English version and the local language version books are available side by side for preparation. Infact, by 3rd or 4th class, the children start teaching you some of the language terms

       Can I teach only during weekends on Saturday and Sunday?
    Ans: eVidyaloka classes happen within the school premises, during school hours. Hence we require volunteers to provide at least 1 hour in the weekday between 10 am to 4 pm (TN ), 9am to 1pm (JH), 11am to 5 pm (KA) and 9am to 4pm (for AP). The second 1 hour could be on a Saturday and shall be worked out during the class planning discussion with the Class Administrators.

       I m a working professional. How can I give teach during this time ?
    Ans: More 60% of current teaching volunteers work full time in their professions. They adopt typicall any of these 3 approaches: 1) Take take the earliest slot at 9am or 10 am and head to work on these two days. 2) They check with their organisation/supervisor to take these two one hour slots for volunteering. And typically no one is being refused, provided the networking infrastructure supports 3) They manage one of the slots around lunch time and another one on a Saturday.

       Do you provide teaching resources and any trainings to teach?
    Ans: Yes. A curriculum plan of recommended topics will provided along with the Timetable. The reference to the Online version of the text books. We are also in the process of developing additional resources like videos, worksheets etc and will be shared as and when available through our content platform Wikividya. Regarding trainings, we have put together certain basic training resources under My Trainings. We encourage all the recommended Teachers to go through the resources and equip themselves with enhanced Teaching capabilities, prior to the class start.

       Do I have to have a physical blackboard for teaching?How is the blackboard need addressed in this online teaching model?
    Ans: The screen share feature in Skype allows the teacher to share their desktop. You can open the Paint, Powerpoint of MS Word and use it as a Whiteboard. What ever is written will be available as display in the 32 screen for the children. In fact this is even more powerful, where you can play Videos, show pictures and the class becomes a truly smart classroom.
